CANYONING:
THE DEPTHS OF ASTURIAS

Leap into the unknown. Rappel down vertical waterfalls and swim through ancient limestone corridors.

The Experience

DESCEND INTO
UNTOUCHED WILDERNESS.

This isn't a walk in the park. Canyoning in Asturias is a raw, sensory-overload journey through the planet's natural plumbing. You'll navigate narrow slots carved over millennia, using ropes to descend gushing waterfalls and sliding down natural stone chutes into emerald pools.

Our expert guides ensure your safety while pushing your limits in the stunning Picos de Europa surroundings.

FAQ

What should I bring? expand_more
Just a swimsuit and a towel. We provide all professional gear: wetsuits, helmets, harnesses, and specialized canyoning boots.
Is it safe if I can't swim? expand_more
Basic swimming ability is recommended for confidence, but our high-buoyancy wetsuits keep you afloat effortlessly. Your guide will assist you in all water sections.
Do I need previous experience? expand_more
Not for our level 1 and 2 canyons. We'll teach you everything you need to know about rappelling and safety on-site.
